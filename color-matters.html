Color Matters

7 Mar 2017
Tweet

Color sets a mood. It speaks to who you are, how you feel, and where you're going. Color is one of the first things you notice when you walk into any room or store. The reason why we eat (looking at you green veggies) and drink certain things are based off color alone. Think about your favorite belongings. It could be a pair of shoes, jeans, hat, car, magazine, cover art, cartoon or video game character. Your favorite belongings are heavily influenced by the color of the product itself. Imagine the Boston Celtics colors being red and white or the Dallas Cowboys blue and orange. You couldn't! Well you could, but that just doesn't feel right. Their colors are apart of their brand identity and picking the right color to represent you, your product, or business is key. There are no wrong colors. What matters most is how you use them.
