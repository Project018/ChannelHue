Channel Hue App: Beta 1.0 is Here

24 Jan 2017
Tweet

The Channel Hue app is now available for free in the iTunes App Store. Here at Channel Hue, we firmly believe in the phrase, "Build And Share," and this app is our first offering. Enjoy.

Beta 1.0 - Here's what you need to know:

  • The Channel Hue app is a collection of colors for users to quickly find, and use. The app is intended to assist with your creative and design needs. Consider it a digital crayon box, housing hundreds of your favorite colors.
  • There are plenty of colors for you to experiment with. Currently, the collection is 300+.
  • We will be updating the collection periodically.
  • Use the search tool to find colors by name, brand, or school.
  • Save your favorite colors on the app and share them via social media, email, and more.
  • Stay tuned for annoucements on the next updates for the project.

We would love to hear what you think in the iTunes App Store and on Twitter @ChannelHue.
